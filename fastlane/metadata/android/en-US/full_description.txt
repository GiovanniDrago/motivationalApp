Motivational App is a tiny ironic joke app built around one very specific idea:
type your name, tap the button, and receive a piece of extremely direct
motivation.

It is minimal on purpose. No account. No feed. No subscriptions. No fake
productivity promises. Just a small, fast, deliberately rude experience that is
more interested in making you laugh than in fixing your life.

Features:
- one quick interaction with instant payoff
- English and Italian localization
- a lightweight About section with project links
- clean interface and fast startup

This app does not pretend to be a mindfulness coach, a life planner, or a
wellness platform. It knows exactly how silly it is.

If you enjoy dry humor, absurdly aggressive encouragement, and small apps with a
clear joke, this one is for you.
